About Us

Open source community Africa (O.S.C.A) was founded in 2018 for open source lovers, enthusiasts, advocates and experts within and across africa with the sole aim of growing or causing an increase in the rate of credible contribution by African software developers, designers, etc to open sourced projects both locally and globally whilst changing the conception of Africans being just next billion users but also the NEXT BILLION CREATORS.
